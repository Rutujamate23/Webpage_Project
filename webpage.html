History

The Ajanta and Ellora Caves are UNESCO World Heritage Sites known for their stunning rock-cut architecture and ancient art.

Attractions

  • Ajanta Caves: Famous for their ancient Buddhist murals.
  • Ellora Caves: Known for their impressive sculptures and carvings.
  • Key attractions include Cave 16, the Kailasa Temple, and more.

Visitor Information

Location: Maharashtra, India

Opening Hours: 9 AM - 5 PM

Entry Fee: ₹40 for Indians, ₹600 for foreigners.
